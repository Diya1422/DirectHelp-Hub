Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How does DirectHelp Hub work?

Fill out the request form with your name, WhatsApp number, category, and request details. On submit, your request is formatted and sent to the admin's WhatsApp. The admin then contacts you personally to provide tailored guidance.

Is this service free?

Yes! DirectHelp Hub provides free support and guidance. Our mission is to make quality guidance accessible to everyone, especially students and early-career professionals.

How long does it take to get a response?

Our admin typically responds within 24 hours. For urgent requests, mention "URGENT" in your message. Response times may vary with request volume.

Is my information kept private?

Absolutely. Your personal information is only used to provide the requested guidance. We do not share your data with third parties.

What if my question doesn't fit any category?

Select "Other Help" from the category dropdown. Our admin handles a wide range of requests, so don't hesitate to ask even if it seems unusual.

Can I submit multiple requests?

Yes, you can submit as many requests as you need. Provide detailed information in each to help us give you the most accurate and useful response.
